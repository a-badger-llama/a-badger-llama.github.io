Sometimes, I let the silence sing

because I have nothing to say,

and I want to listen

outside myself.

A cool wind whispers wisdom

from that place between places,

and this fearful face is erased.

I embrace every doubt,

and doubt that too,

because then I know that

I don’t know anything.

That’s when courage finds me

to excite and invite me

into movement,

and new experience.

Without a word,

I bear witness.

And like the wind,

I breathe what I see.

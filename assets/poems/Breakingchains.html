My mind is so busy, it’s blank,

and I’m intent on being distracted.

Can’t break these chains, so

I just cope.

Doubt compounds to debilitate

and create a cage, but at least

I’m safe.

Protected from passion

that shakes my foundation.

Let it pass by

out of reach.

Confined, I fill time

with stories I tell myself.

Dreams I see when I close my eyes.

Let me sleep.

Until nightmares arrive and I find

life seeping from me

into the dirt I wallow in.

Time to break the chains

and collapse the cage.

Time to chase my dreams

to outrun my nightmares.

Time to live.

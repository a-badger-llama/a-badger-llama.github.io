Hello Anger.

I should have known it was you

by the mad knocking, knocking, knocking

at the door of my mind.

Impossible to deny,

knocking becomes pounding,

becomes violent invasion.

I’m held hostage –

senses hijacked while Reason is

tied up in some maze again.

You came in a flurry of fury

and scattered these

cards I lived in.

An explosion of heat turns

stone walls into open doors

and I’m free.

Thank you for the pain,

Anger, my old friend.

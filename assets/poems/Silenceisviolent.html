And yes,

silence can be violent.

But be brave and face the rage.

Listen to what it has to say.

Because there is no

demon on your shoulder.

Only the mad voice

in your mind,

born from neglect.

And to turn away

is to stop your ears

and shut your eyes

when the fight finds you.

Distraction is not escape,

and painkillers can't stop the bleeding.

What you need is healing.

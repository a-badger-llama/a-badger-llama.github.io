As the sun rises

I begin to dream.

Passion wants what it wants.

That agent of the heart

urges action.

It is the white heat

That shapes metal

I will temper this.

Make it safe

for you to hold.

But temperance alone is cold.

Stoke the flame that

fuels my devotion.

Grant the ability

to see the future,

for I’ve begun to dream.

Build with me a vehicle

that we may cross

great divides

to find new planes.

A new day

orbiting another star.

Who will we be here?

Do not fear;

wield your power.

I will take care

to guard my heart.

But you and God know,

I’ve begun to dream.

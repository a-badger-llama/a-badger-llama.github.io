Just me by the fire.

My voice is swallowed,

so I let my thoughts wander

and they echo back flawless.

It’s as if something in the air I breath

makes me feel all these things.

That’s magic.

And if me being me

moves you,

then I am not sorry.

Motion is medicine.

Because everyone gets stuck

– trapped in our own pattern’s disasters.

Let the chains fall.

That’s magic.

Sometimes chains are just avoided choices

and there is still life to live.

So make your choice.

Give me liberty or give me death,

because I won't stand for less.

You can't choose life without thinking about death.

I do on days when life hurts

and realize this too,

I shall survive.

That’s magic.

I can always change,

so long as I’m willing to

go through with the pain of it.

I commit,

and the rest

just resolves.

That's magic.
